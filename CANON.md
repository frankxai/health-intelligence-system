# CANON - Health Intelligence System

This repo defines no medical canon.

It adopts the following operating canon:

- Evidence over vibes.
- Clinical humility over certainty theater.
- Decision preparation over decision replacement.
- Source freshness over memory.
- Privacy over convenience.

No health framework in this repo should be treated as clinical authority. Treating clinicians and current guideline bodies hold clinical authority.
