# Review Gate

`v0.1.0` is allowed to ship as a public prerelease for open review and adoption testing. It must not be marketed as production-ready clinical material until this gate is closed.

## Required Review

| Gate | Status | Reviewer | Date | Notes |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Medical safety review | pending | TBD | TBD | Claims, disclaimers, emergency routing, screening language |
| Legal/privacy review | pending | TBD | TBD | Medical disclaimer, privacy language, public/private boundary |
| UX comprehension review | pending | TBD | TBD | First-time user can tell this is prep, not care |

## Promotion Rule

Before removing prerelease status:

- all gate statuses must be `passed`;
- reviewer names or roles must be recorded;
- changes requested by reviewers must be implemented;
- `SAFETY.md`, `PRIVACY.md`, and `docs/evidence-sources.md` must be rechecked.
