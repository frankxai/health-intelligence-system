# What This Is Not

**Evidence checked:** 2026-06-22  
**Scope:** plain-language boundary for Health Intelligence System.  
**Safety boundary:** If symptoms, abnormal results, medication questions, or urgent concerns are involved, use qualified clinical care. This page is not medical advice.

## Not A Doctor

Health Intelligence System is not a doctor, nurse, pharmacist, therapist, dietitian, trainer, emergency service, insurer, or legal advisor.

It helps organize:

- records;
- timelines;
- questions;
- routines;
- source ledgers;
- appointment prep;
- clinician-facing summaries.

It does not make medical decisions.

## Not A Diagnosis Engine

Do not use HIS to decide what condition someone has.

Allowed:

- "Here is the timeline in my own words."
- "Here are the records I have."
- "What questions should I ask my clinician?"
- "What public education sources explain this term?"

Not allowed:

- "Diagnose this."
- "Rule out cancer."
- "Interpret this scan."
- "Tell me what this lab means for me."

## Not A Treatment Recommender

Do not use HIS to choose, rank, delay, start, stop, or change treatment.

Allowed:

- organize options a clinician already presented;
- list side effects to ask about;
- prepare second-opinion packet;
- track follow-up responsibilities.

Not allowed:

- recommend regimen;
- recommend medication;
- recommend supplement;
- recommend fasting;
- recommend alternative protocol;
- recommend delay or refusal of care.

## Not Emergency Triage

Do not use HIS to decide whether severe or rapidly changing symptoms are urgent.

Use local emergency services, urgent care, or the care team's urgent-contact instructions.

## Not A Public Health Record Store

This public repo must not contain real private health data.

Use a private vault outside public Git for:

- PDFs;
- lab reports;
- portal exports;
- medication lists;
- appointment notes;
- identifiers;
- insurance data;
- family history.

## Not A Compliance Shortcut

Local files, private GitHub, encrypted folders, ChatGPT Projects, ChatGPT Health, enterprise tools, or self-hosted LLMs do not automatically make a system HIPAA, GDPR, EHDS, or locally compliant.

Treat compliance as a professional review track.
