# Built On And Inspired By

**Evidence checked:** 2026-06-22  
**Scope:** public positioning for Health Intelligence System.  
**Safety boundary:** This page explains influences and boundaries. It is not medical, legal, privacy, security, or compliance advice.

## Positioning

Health Intelligence System is not presented as the first AI health assistant, the best medical model, or a replacement for clinical care.

It is a practical protocol for people who need to organize health records, prepare doctor visits, manage nutrition and fitness logs, understand sources, and choose an AI/privacy setup without losing control of their private record.

The distinctive design is the combination of:

- local-first markdown records;
- explicit public/private data tiers;
- clinician-handoff workflows;
- jurisdiction-aware record-access adapters;
- optional ChatGPT, Custom GPT, local LLM, and coding-agent modes;
- strict refusal to diagnose, prescribe, or rank treatment.

## What Is Unique Here

The uniqueness claim should stay narrow and defensible:

> Health Intelligence System is a markdown-first, privacy-tiered, clinician-handoff protocol for personal health operations that can be run with ChatGPT, Custom GPTs, local LLMs, Obsidian, Notion, Git-safe templates, and coding agents while keeping raw health records outside public repositories.

Do not claim:

- best medical AI;
- clinical accuracy;
- diagnosis;
- treatment recommendation;
- HIPAA/GDPR compliance by default;
- guaranteed privacy merely because a tool is local.

## Attribution Practice

Every public artifact that references an external system should include:

- source URL;
- date accessed;
- what HIS learned from it;
- what HIS will not copy;
- whether the source is a product, research paper, standard, open-source repo, or curated list.
