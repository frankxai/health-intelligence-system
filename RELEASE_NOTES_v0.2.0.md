# Health Intelligence System v0.2.0

Preclinical public prerelease for personal health operations, clinician-safe workflows, and Agentic Life OS integration.

## New

- Added Personal Health Operations v0.2 docs, commands, and templates.
- Added public/private architecture for private vaults, AI use, clinician handoff, and research companion boundaries.
- Added product boundary between public HIS, private Agentic Life OS, Agentic Health OS, and research-only Life Sciences Researcher IS.
- Added Codex plugin package for a sovereign health operator skill.
- Added external systems comparison, repo consolidation map, jurisdiction model, and source-backed safety/privacy model.
- Expanded clinician handoff, doctor-visit prep, disease-navigation, nutrition/fitness, medication/supplement inventory, and private-vault templates.

## Safety Status

This remains a preclinical prerelease. It does not diagnose, interpret labs or imaging, prescribe treatment, recommend medication or supplement changes, or replace clinicians.
