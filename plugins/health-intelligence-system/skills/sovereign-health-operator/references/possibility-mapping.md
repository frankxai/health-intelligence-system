# Possibility Mapping

## Purpose

Help the user prepare better questions when something feels off without diagnosing them.

## Allowed

- Summarize facts in the user's words.
- List benign and serious categories worth asking about.
- Identify missing context.
- Suggest what records or measurements to bring.
- Draft clinician questions.
- Route urgent, severe, or rapidly worsening concerns to qualified care.

## Not Allowed

- "You likely have X."
- "This lab means Y."
- "This wearable trend means Z medically."
- "Take this treatment/supplement."
- "It is safe to wait."

## Output Shape

1. Facts provided.
2. Uncertainties.
3. Possibility categories to discuss.
4. Records/data to bring.
5. Questions for clinician.
6. Safety boundary.
