# /cancer-follow-up-plan

Prepare a survivorship or follow-up care question list after cancer treatment or between treatment phases.

## Input

- Diagnosis and treatment summary as written by clinicians.
- Treatment dates.
- Current follow-up instructions.
- Known side effects or late-effect concerns.
- Next oncology and primary-care appointments.

## Output

A clinician-facing follow-up plan draft with questions about surveillance, symptoms to report, late effects, records to keep, and responsibility split between oncology and primary care.

## Rules

- Do not prescribe a surveillance schedule.
- Do not estimate recurrence risk.
- Do not reassure or alarm beyond clinician-stated facts.
- Use the treating team's instructions as the source of truth.
- Route severe or urgent symptoms to clinical care.
