# /health-optimization-weekly-review

Review one week of private health logs and wearable summaries for ordinary wellness optimization.

## Input

- Weekly nutrition/fitness loop.
- Wearable summary, if available.
- Sleep and recovery notes.
- Energy, mood, stress, soreness, symptoms, and travel notes.
- Current goals and constraints.
- Professional guidance already received.

## Output

Use `templates/health-operator-weekly-review.md`.

Include:

- patterns noticed;
- possible confounders;
- low-risk next-week wellness experiment;
- questions for clinician, dietitian, trainer, therapist, or coach;
- missing data;
- privacy check.

## Rules

- Do not prescribe diet therapy, supplements, medication changes, fasting, rehab, or treatment.
- Do not infer disease from logs or wearable data.
- Avoid certainty. Use "pattern to watch" and "question to ask" language.
- Escalate symptoms, abnormal results, severe issues, or rapid worsening to qualified care.
