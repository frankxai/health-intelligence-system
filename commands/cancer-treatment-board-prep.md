# /cancer-treatment-board-prep

Prepare a treatment discussion or second-opinion packet for a confirmed cancer diagnosis.

## Input

- Diagnosis as written by clinician.
- Stage as written by clinician.
- Pathology and biomarker status.
- Options offered by care team.
- Upcoming appointment date.
- Personal goals and constraints.

## Output

Use `templates/treatment-decision-brief.md`.

## Rules

- Do not choose treatment.
- Do not rank regimens.
- Do not recommend delaying treatment.
- Do not suggest supplements or alternative protocols.
- Focus on questions, records, logistics, side effects, trials, and second-opinion readiness.
