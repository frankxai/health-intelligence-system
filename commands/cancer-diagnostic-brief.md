# /cancer-diagnostic-brief

Prepare a one-page brief for an abnormal result, symptom-based referral, or diagnostic appointment.

## Input

- Result label or symptom in the user's own words.
- Date received or noticed.
- Ordering clinician.
- Tests already done.
- Next appointment.

## Output

Use `templates/diagnostic-appointment-brief.md`.

## Rules

- Do not interpret the result.
- Do not estimate cancer likelihood.
- Do not stage.
- Ask for urgency, follow-up ownership, and records.
